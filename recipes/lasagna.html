Beef Lasagna

  • Beef mince 1kg
  • Fresh lasagna sheets
  • 1 large white onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Seasoning and spices of your choice

Steps

  1. Add oil of your choice to the pan and add your onion and 2 garlic cloves/li>
  2. Once your onions have been cooked through add in your beef mince
  3. Cook 3/4 of the way through as you don't want to overcook your mince as you will be oven baking this later
  4. Now layer your lasagna with your sheets, top with cheese and oven bake for 40 minutes on medium
  5. Cool for 10 minutes and enjoy, bon appetit!

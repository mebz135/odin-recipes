Scrambled Eggs

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 30ml of whole milk
  1. Get your pan on a medium heat and add your butter in as well
  2. Whilst the pan is warming up crack 3 eggs in a bowl pinch of salt and dash of pepper, add in the whole milk
  3. Add egg mixture into pan and bring the heat down slightly
  4. Bring the eggs in from the side of the pan and scramble
  5. Plate up with a side of toast
  6. Enjoy!

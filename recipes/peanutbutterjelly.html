Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • Skippy's peanut butter
  • Strawberry Jelly
  1. Place both slices of white bread and spread generously with peanut butter and jelly, 1 side for peanut butter and the other for jelly
  2. Add to pre warmed pan and toast till both sides are golden brown
  3. Enjoy!
